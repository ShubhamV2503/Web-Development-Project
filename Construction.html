Construction detail of Nagarjuna Sagar Dam.

Data

The construction of the Nagarjunasagar Dam was commenced in the year 1956 and completed by the year 1967. It is one of the Modern Temples of Free India and the pride of Andhra Pradesh. This is the largest and highest masonry dam in the world. The total length of Dam is 15956 Ft (4863 mts), with a Full Storage Capacity of 408.24 TMC & Live Storage of 202.47 TMC. The F.R.L. of the Dam is 590.00 Ft., and the water spread area of the Dam is 285 Sq. Km. There are 26 Nos. of Radial Crest Gates with a Spillway capacity of 42,476 cumecs. Water was let into the Right Canal by Smt. Indira Gandhi on 4.8.1967
